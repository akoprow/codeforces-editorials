Educational Codeforces Round 87 (Rated for Div. 2)

1354A Alarm Clock [CF] (Code) R:900 math

Fairly simple arithmetic; just be careful thinking through the cases.

1354B Ternary String [CF] (Code) R:1200 binary search dp implementation two pointers

For all of \(1, 2, 3\) create a set with their positions. Then iterate through positions of \(1\) and find closest \(2\) to the left and right; same for \(3\) (they may not exist) and then just pick an overall best.

1354C1 Simple Polygon Embedding [CF] (Code) R:1400 binary search geometry math ternary search

Helps to know how to compute circumradius and apothem in a regular polygon. In this case things are fairly regular so it’s relatively simple trigonometry.

1354C2 Not So Simple Polygon Embedding [CF] (Code) R:2000 binary search brute force geometry math

In this case things are not as regular as in C1. It’s possibly to do a binary search over possible rotations or just observe that due to symmetry “half” the rotation between “regular” rotations is the answer. See bottom of the notes for a “visual explanation”.
